About Our Company

Valkron Forge was founded in 2019 by Mathew Downie, who started the company in a small workshop after getting frustrated with overpriced, poorly assembled prebuilt PCs dominating the market. What began as a one-man operation building systems for local gamers quickly grew as word spread about Mathew’s obsession with clean craftsmanship, thermal efficiency, and zero-bloat performance. Within the first year, Valkron Forge expanded into a dedicated facility and shipped its first 1,000 systems, setting the foundation for the brand’s reputation as a premium yet honest PC builder.

Today, Valkron Forge has scaled into a full engineering and production team while staying grounded in its original values of transparency, quality parts, and enthusiast-grade performance. Each build goes through a 47-point inspection process, including thermal profiling, firmware optimization, and real-world stress testing to ensure reliability before it ever reaches a customer. With a rapidly growing community and a commitment to continuous improvement, Valkron Forge stands as a modern benchmark for what a prebuilt PC company should be performance focused, user driven, and built with purpose.
